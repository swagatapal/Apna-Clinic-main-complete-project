Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is a cancer that forms in cells. After skin cancer, breast cancer is the most common cancer diagnosed in women in the United States. Breast cancer can occur in both men and women, but it's far more common in women.


Symptoms

  • A breast lump or thickening that feels different from the surrounding tissue
  • Change in the size, shape or appearance of the breast
  • Changes to the skin over the breast, such as dimpling
  • Redness or ptting of the skin over your breast, like the skin of an orange
